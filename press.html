Our Story

Hack Horizon is a unique TravelTech hackathon taking place at 30,000 ft, on a public flight from Hong Kong to London.

Located within a 5-hour flight from 50% of world’s population, Hong Kong is at the heart of Asia and has the potential to benefit economically and socially from the growth of travel industry. Hack Horizon will bring the best innovators and key industry players together to galvanise Hong Kong and put it on the map as a TravelTech hub.

We will hand-pick and challenge 32 of the best entrepreneurs, engineers and designers to build unique products and services that focus on improving the end-to-end customer experience in international travel. Teams will focus work 6 key verticals: insurance, airports, transportation, accommodation, bookings and airlines, whilst iterating their products with traveling customers in real-time at HKIA and Heathrow airport.

Unlike other hackathons, Hack Horizon has also created a sustainable framework for innovation beyond the main event by offering the best teams a platform to grow their ideas with the access to the current market leaders, investors, advisors and media.

Hack Horizon, ready for departure March 2017.

Our Community

We are passionate and dedicated to making TravelTech a recognised technology vertical in the startup world and getting the established travel industry to embrace technology better.

To foster the local TravelTech ecosystems across Hong Kong and London, we are running monthly TravelTech Tuesdays panel discussions and meet-up events. We consistently attract 100+ attendees from across major travel companies, media, startups, students and the general public.
