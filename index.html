The First of Its Kind

build. discover. soar

Hack Horizon will break down the barriers created between industries and countries to build opportunities and enable collaboration between two of the largest and most powerful startup ecosystems in the world.

Design Thinking will unlock consumer facing innovation, as the teams are able to interview and empathise with real life customers in travel, while strategic partnerships across 6 key pillars in the travel industry will give us the rich insights, data and technology we need to unlock new products and services for business.

Horizon will also bring on board a range of technology partners who will work with us across the entire travel industry to give the teams the software, data, API’s and insights they will need to build robust TravelTech prototypes.

The Journey

Horizon will help unite the Hong Kong and London startup ecosystems, while showcasing Hong Kong as a worthy home of innovation, talent and tech in the global landscape.

Ideation

By bringing together leading industry experts, participants will have the exclusive opportunity to build a holistic understanding of the travel industry.

Research

To truly understand the challenges they are trying to address, participants will be able to directly interact with their customers at the Hong Kong International Airport.

Prototyping

By experiencing the same journey and process as a typical traveler, participants will be able to empathise with and discover the problems at the heart of international travel.

Validation

Unlike other hackathons where products are build in a vacuum, participants will get the chance to test and validate their prototypes with real potential customers at Heathrow Airport.

Pitch

Participants will have the opportunity
to pitch in front of industry experts at London’s new TravelTech co-working space.
