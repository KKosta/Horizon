What is Hack Horizon

Be one of the chosen 32 and join the best engineers, developers and designers from across APAC on an extraordinary journey.

Embark on a unique hackathon where we will give you 3 days and one flight from Hong Kong to London to build new products that can make travel simpler, safer, cheaper and way more fun.

Unlike other hackathons, Hack Horizon will completely immerse you in the travel experience and give you access to real life customers to test your assumptions right off the bat. What’s more is, that you will be granted special access to some of the best travel technologies and APIs out there as well as have the support and mentorship of leading industry experts.

So jump on board and join us for this once in a lifetime, adrenaline-fueled travel experience.

The Hackathon

Hack Horizon Horizon will help unite the Hong Kong and London startup ecosystems, while showcasing Hong Kong as a worthy home of innovation, talent and tech in the global landscape.

Day 1 | Hong Kong

Hackathon Kick Off

We will kick the hackathon off in Hong Kong on the first evening with a warm welcome by the team and key partners and sponsors. You will pitch ideas, form teams and begin developing and building your prototypes with the support of expert mentors from across the travel industry.

HK Airport Express

We will close off the day speeding towards Hong Kong International Airport on the HK Airport Express and set up camp at an airport hotel to get some rest before the flight on Day 2.

Day 2 | The Airport + Flight

Hong Kong International

On Day 2, we will head to Hong Kong International Airport, where we will get a behind the scenes tour of how the airport operates and a chance to do some customer testing and validation with real passengers.

The flight

In the evening we will board the British Airways flight on an A380 from Hong Kong to London where you will consolidate everything you've learnt at the airport, as get some well deserved rest.

Day 3 | The Airport + London

Heathrow Airport

We land at London Heathrow Airport early in the morning where you will set up and have another opportunity to do customer validation with passengers. At lunchtime we will head into London on the Heathrow Express.

TravelTech Lab

We will spend the afternoon and evening working from London's TravelTech Lab where a range of mentors will come down to give feedback and support you in the final stage of developing your prototypes.

Day 4 | London + Transport Museum

London

On the last day we head back to the TravelTech Lab for a final day of hacking and you will have a chance to practice your pitch and demo.

Pitch Finals

We end the hackathon at the iconic London Transport Museum where we will be joined by travel industry leaders, serial entrepreneurs, press, media and an expert panel of judges, whom you will be pitching in-front of.

JUmp on Board

Every single Hack Horizon participant will be handpicked and will go through an extensive recruitment process to make sure we bring the right talent and skills on board to truly change the face of travel.

Apply

All applicants will submit a short form where we can begin identifying the most talented, creative, passionate and driven individuals.

Video Pitch

We will shortlist the top 250 applicants and ask them to submit a 60 second video elevator pitch on why they should be selected, so we can see how they look off paper.

Interview

We will select the top 100 video pitches who will move to the final stage, where we will have a short interview with each applicant before selecting the final 32.
