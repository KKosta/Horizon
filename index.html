Connecting cities

Hong Kong

Before take-off industry experts and mentors will prepare you for the journey. Everything from industry pain points to how to create effective customer surveys will be covered. Once at the airport you will have time to validate your ideas with real customers.

London

During the flight you will have the opportunity to consilate your user feedback and start prototyping your products. Upon touching down we will be connecting with the local startup and travel ecosystem and have one final night to bring it all together for your pitch and demo in front of the judges and a live audience the following day.

2017 will be the year of travel

STARTUPS ARE DOMINATING...

Uber and AirBnB are the largest companies in the world for taxis and accommodation but startups like Hotel Tonight and Kayak are quickly shaking up other parts of the travel industry too.

MILLENNIALS ARE TRAVELING...

88% of 18 – 30 year olds now travel internationally one to three times a year in search of new and unique experiences and we need new travel services and products to address this growing market.

TECH IS TAKING OVER...

From online bookings to epassports and inflight WiFi the nearly 200M passengers who pass through HK International, Heathrow and JFK every year have never been more digital, connected and mobile.

Personalisation is key...

Building great user experiences has never been more important and with Design Thinking to itterate with customers in real time, so they can solve the real pain points in travel.
